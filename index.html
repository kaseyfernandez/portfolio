About

Hello world! I am KM Fern a web devloper and designer located in NE Portland, OR. I have experience with HTML, CSS, UX/ UI Design, Python and Javascript.

Web development is a second career for me. While teaching Tech and Library Media classes to K-8 students I discovered a passion for coding, gamification and digital art. I truly believe that digital development is the profession of the future.

Although I miss story time and introducing students to emerging technology, I am ecstatic to branch into a profession that allows me to use my creativity in full force. Coding and digital art allows my dreams to become a visible reality. It's simply magic!

When composing content and layout my end users are at the forefront of my mind. I aim to build intuitive websites that deliver relevant standards based content with minimal explanation and a bit of fun.
