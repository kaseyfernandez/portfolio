KM Fern aka Kasey Fernandez

Web Developer and Instructional Designer

Hello world! I am KM Fern a web devloper and designer located in NE Portland, OR. I have experience with HTML, CSS, UX/ UI Design, Python and Javascript.

Web development is a second career for me. While teaching Tech and Library Media classes to K-8 students I discovered a passion for coding, gamification and digital art. I truly believe that digital development is the brightest profession of the future.

When composing content and layout my end users are at the forefront of my mind. I aim to build websites that deliver relevant, important content in an intuitive manner that requires minimal explanation and a bit of fun.
